Join the Feelgood February Challenge!

With the new year, you might have noticed your social feeds filled with new diets, exercise programmes and other regimes. But this is a challenge with a difference: it’s not a regime, it’s about giving yourself space to reflect on who you are and what’s important to you.

Our thoughts affect our feelings, which affect our behaviours. So we know that real, lasting change begins in our brains! By taking time to look within and detox our mind, we can set ourselves up for the year ahead.

So … what’s the challenge?

Each week we will look at a different aspect of wellbeing:

Week One: Thinking about our Thinking

Week Two: Our relationships with others

Week Three: Our relationship with ourselves

Week Four: Fun things to support self-care

Watch out for our blog post each Monday to learn more about each topic, and follow the weekly challenge. We’ll also be posting smaller, daily challenges throughout the week on Facebook - so make sure you're following Just a Thought on Facie!
